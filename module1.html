CN110426298A - 2019/11/08 Utility Model Relates Technical Field Punch the Card Fixed Connection

Testing material low-temp toughness composite set

The invention discloses a testing material low-temperature toughness composite set. The composite set comprises an thermal insulation box body and a testing box body; the thermal insulation box body is a pentahedron box body structure and provided with a thermal insulation layer; the testing box body is arranged in the thermal insulation box body; a cavity is formed between the testing box body and the thermal insulation box body; an inlet medium hole and an outlet medium hole are arranged at an upper location of a sidewall of the thermal insulation box body, the appropriate low-temperature medium is injected into the thermal insulation box body, thereby creating the low-temperature experimental environment for the testing box; a clamp is arranged in the testing box, the clamp clamps a sample material; a punch is arranged at the upper side in the testing box body to connect a connecting rod so as to perform the impact test; the punch regulates the height through buffer springs, guide rods and a scale plate so as to adjust the impact strength to guarantee the impact test.		 JIANGMEN ZHENYI INDUSTRIAL CO., LTD SI HAORAN WANG YIMAI WEI JINCHUAN SHAO HAILEI XU HONGSHEN
